Welcome to Early Childhood Support Resource

This is the Early Childhood Support Resource which is a professional site offering support to educators in the confusing and sometimes challenging types of situations that children and families in the modern society encounter. The resource examines the diverse family, and community settings that may immensely influence the development, welfare, and participation of children in the early learning curriculum.
